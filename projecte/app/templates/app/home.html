Gymkhanr

Welcome to Gymkhanr

Gymkhanr allows you to easily create and organise gymkhanas, treasure hunts, and any other activities you may think of, and also lets the players see their progress and their current objectives at a glance.

Create gymkhanas easily

Gymkhanr lets you create two simple but powerful types of tests: Questions and Challenges. Questions can have a lot of answers you can choose, but only one of them is correct! Meanwhile, Challenges can only be completed when you input a code, given to you by the creator of the gymkhana.
